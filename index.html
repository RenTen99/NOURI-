Why We Built This

We are not in the vending business.

Some ideas begin with a problem. NOURIÉ began with a standard — the belief that every great space deserves amenities that reflect the quality of the space itself. Walking into a beautifully designed office, luxury apartment, or boutique gym and encountering a dated vending machine in the corner is a missed opportunity. One that communicates the opposite of everything the property was designed to convey.

NOURIÉ was created to eliminate that gap. We are a curated workplace amenity company — not a vending operator. The distinction matters in every decision we make: which products we carry, how we design our stations, how we service our partners, and how we position our company for long-term growth including government and institutional contracting.

Based in Houston, Texas. Veteran-owned. Built on the belief that business can be both principled and exceptional.
