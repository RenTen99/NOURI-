Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Is there really no cost to my business?+
Correct. NOURIÉ provides the station, installation, product stocking, weekly restocking, and full maintenance at no charge. We earn revenue from product sales and share a percentage with you monthly.
How is the revenue share calculated?+
Revenue share is a percentage of total net sales each calendar month. Payments are issued monthly via ACH or check with a detailed statement by the 15th.
What happens after the 30-day free trial?+
We review performance together. If it's not working, we remove the station at no cost. If it's working, we continue under standard 90-day terms.
How much floor space does the station require?+
Standard footprint is approximately 24" wide × 30" deep — smaller than most office printers. We confirm placement during the discovery visit.
Can I choose which products go in my station?+
Yes. During the discovery visit we learn about your clientele and propose a product mix — you have approval before installation.
Do I need to do anything after installation?+
Nothing. NOURIÉ handles all restocking and maintenance weekly. Contact your account manager if anything needs attention between visits.

Our Story

The NOURIÉ Concept

Born from the belief that every great space deserves amenities that match its standard.

Why We Built This

Wellness-forward convenience
for modern spaces.

Some ideas begin with a problem. NOURIÉ began with a feeling — walking into a beautiful, carefully designed space and encountering a vending machine in the corner. Loud branding. Generic products. A machine that communicated the opposite of everything the business had worked to build.

NOURIÉ was created to solve that disconnect. We believe every great space deserves amenities that match its standard — not generic convenience, not an afterthought. A curated, beautifully designed self-service experience that feels like it was always meant to be there.

We are not in the vending business. We are in the business of elevating spaces.

Premium
We source exclusively from wellness and gourmet brands clients recognize and trust. Every item is intentional.
Minimal
Our station design and operational model are built around simplicity. Visually clean. Quietly functional.
Hospitality
We approach every partnership the way a boutique hotel approaches its guest experience — with genuine care.
